Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic information such as your name, email address, and an encrypted version of your password. We do not collect or store any payment card details, bank account numbers, or government-issued identifiers.

As you use the Service, we record the expense data you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and notes — along with limited technical information such as your browser type, device, and the pages you visit on Spendly.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Service: to let you log in, view and analyze your expenses, send you essential account notifications, and keep Spendly secure and reliable.

We never sell your personal information. We do not use your expense data to build advertising profiles, and we do not share your records with marketers or data brokers.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers and protected with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Passwords are stored only as one-way hashes; we cannot read or recover your original password.

You may export or permanently delete your data at any time from your account settings. Deleted records are removed from our active systems immediately and from backups within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of trusted providers for hosting, email delivery, and error monitoring. These providers process data only on our behalf and are bound by contractual obligations to safeguard your information.

We do not integrate advertising networks or social trackers into the Service. If we ever add an optional integration — such as importing transactions from your bank — it will be clearly opt-in and described before you enable it.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to access or delete your data, or would like to raise a concern, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app.

We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within seven business days.
